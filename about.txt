### about %(name)s

%(name)s is a protocol handler for %(protocol)s(s) URIs, which are
used to address virtual worlds --- for example,

> [rezzme://www.osgrid.org:8002/](rezzme://www.osgrid.org:8002/)

will log you in to [OSgrid](http://www.osgrid.org/).
